About Me

I grew up in San Antonio Texas. In high school my hobbies were art and playing tennis and lacrosse. I originally went to school at the University of North Texas, where I majored in Communication Design. I then chose to pursue the Disney College Program for a semester before transferring to UTSA, where I majored in Sport, Event, and Tourism Management.

Since graduating, I have worked several jobs in the sports and tourism industry. I am the assistant photographer for the Alamo Bowl, I worked as coaches video for the San Antonio Commanders, and as a travel counselor for Visit San Antonio.
